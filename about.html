About This Project

This project publicly confirms the validity of Goldbach’s Conjecture up to very large even numbers. It is based on a novel method developed by Bahbouhi Bouchaib, an independent researcher based in Nantes, France.

The method used, called the GPS Method, is a predictive technique that selects specific values of p such that both p and q = E − p are prime numbers for a given even number E. The method has been tested and successfully verified for even numbers up to 1010000.

This archive provides selected results only. The work is original and offered as a public contribution to mathematics. It is not a calculation tool. All results have been precomputed using the GPS strategy and manually verified.

For additional information or citations, you may refer to the associated research articles under review or published on open-access platforms.
